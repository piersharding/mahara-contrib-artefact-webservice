Plugin Administration - Webservices

The rough order of configuration for webservices, is in the flow top to bottom of the page.

  • Enable or disable all webservices completely
  • Switch on or off individual protocols
  • Manage and/or construct service groups, which are clusters of functions that can be remotely called
  • Manage service access tokens for token based authenticated access to web services
  • Manage users connected to services for username/password based authenticated access to services
